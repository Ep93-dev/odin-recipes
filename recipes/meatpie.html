Meat Pie

This meat pie is delicious!

Beef, pork, and potato are all nicely spiced and baked together. It's yummy and pretty easy to make too! It's best served fresh, but you can also make it ahead and freeze or refrigerate it. Serve the pie by itself or with a beef gravy.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Vegetables
  • Spice and seasonings
  • Water
  • Pie crust

Steps

  1. Boil and mash the potatoes.
  2. Cook the meat with the water, onion, garlic, and seasonings
  3. Mix in the potatoes, then fill the pie crust. Cover with the top crust.
  4. Bake until the crust is browned.
